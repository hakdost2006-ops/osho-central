Donation and Support Policy

Last updated: June 6, 2026

Osho Central support payments are intended to help with platform development, hosting, directory verification, event tools, and Osho Sage development.

Payments made through this site are separate from Osho Meditation Retreat USA payments on Wix. Retreat registration, lodging, and meal payments should continue through the existing retreat links unless otherwise stated.

Support payments are not purchases of Osho books, Osho materials, or official certification. If a payment was made in error, email oshonc369@gmail.com within 14 days and include the payment receipt.

Refunds may depend on the payment processor and the nature of the contribution. Before public launch, every payment link should be tested with a small payment and refund.
