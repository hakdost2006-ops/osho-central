Claim Page

Let center leaders take ownership.

The final site should make claiming simple, while still protecting the directory from wrong or stale updates.

1. Request access

A center leader clicks Claim, enters their name, email, role, website, and a short note proving connection to the center.

2. Admin review

Osho Central checks the request against public website, email domain, social pages, or direct confirmation.

3. Manage profile

Approved managers can update description, address, schedule, events, links, photos, and contact details.
