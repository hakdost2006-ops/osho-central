Claim System

Every imported profile needs ownership.

The same workflow should work globally: import from Osho.com, mark as unverified, invite claim, review, then allow center managers to update their own page.

Imported Profile

Basic name, city, contact, activities, source, and status appear publicly with a clear “Imported from Osho.com” note.

Claim Request

Center leader submits their name, email, role, website/social proof, and correction notes.

Managed Page

Approved leaders update contact info, meditations, events, photos, current schedules, and registration links.
